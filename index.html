What is R?

  1. Used by statistics researchers (made by statisticians initially)
  2. Flexible
  3. Freeware http://www.r-project.org
  4. Widespread
  5. Repository CRAN
  6. Platform independent
  7. Most common editor - RStudio
  8. Handling data is easy (Vector, Matrix, Dataframe, List)
  9. Connects with other languages, applications and databases easily
  10. Highly engaged community - StackOverflow
